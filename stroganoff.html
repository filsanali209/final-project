Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Cooked egg noodles

Method

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef slices and cook until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, saut√© onions and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Gradually add beef broth, stirring constantly until thickened.
  7. Return beef to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in sour cream and cook until heated through. Do not boil.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Serve over cooked egg noodles.
