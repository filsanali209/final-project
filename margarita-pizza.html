Margarita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
