Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, and sour cream for toppings

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef in a pan over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, and paprika.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve beef in taco shells with your choice of toppings.
