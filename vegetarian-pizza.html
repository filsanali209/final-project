Vegetarian Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, and red onions evenly over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil on top.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
