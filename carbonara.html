Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Method

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind.
  3. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated cheeses.
  4. Add salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Fry the pancetta until crispy, then add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the pancetta, then remove from heat.
  7. Quickly mix in the eggs and cheese, adding some cooking water if needed.
  8. Serve with a good grinding of black pepper and extra cheese.
