Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Cooked basmati rice, for serving

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Add chicken, and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook until softened and beginning to brown.
  4. Stir in garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add paprika and garam masala, and cook for 1 minute more.
  6. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer for 10 minutes, until thickened.
  7. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Thread chicken onto skewers, and grill until juices run clear.
  8. Remove chicken from skewers and add to sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with cooked basmati rice.
