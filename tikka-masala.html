Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Method

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, and spices in a bowl. Add chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Bake the chicken for 20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and cook until onions are soft.
  4. Add spices and cook for another minute. Add tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the cream and add the cooked chicken. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
